We facilitate small funding for bottom-up and horizontalist anticapitalist entities in Southern Africa.

Ghostseed is a low-tech collective organised to direct money from the Global North.

Our Process

  • ConnectYour work is engaged by people within our collective or broader network. They report back to us and we see what we can do.
  • ContactWe get in touch with you via the collective email or via text message. We discuss what options there are for us send you money.
  • TransferWe send it. Our only request is that you respect our privacy and security.
