Ghostseed has cultivated
sources of funding in the Global North

Their dollars, euros, etc. go further in Southern Africa.
The money will go towards supporting those who fight domination and build autonomy.

So how does it work?

Connect

We learn of your pre-existing project, organisation, group, or trajectory.

Contact

We get in touch with you via our email, or via text message. We just need your bank's name and your account number. We will not ask for more than this.

We prefer email because our phone number changes regularly but our email stays the same, however we will text your phone if we do not have an email contact.

Transfer

We put cash directly into your account, with no conditions.

Who does Ghostseed support? Many, however:

We prioritise grassroots communities, groups, and individuals who do not have PBO status.
We prioritise organisations led by people who experience the problems the organisations seek to better.
We prioritise bottom-up and flat-structure anticapitalist groups.
